Lucid empowers you to invest collectively with other people.

By investing with your friends you have access to more capital and knowledge -- and it's more fun!

How It Works

1. Form a Group

Get as few or many of your friends or colleagues together. You and you friends can be accredited or non-accredited investors.

2. Gather Capital

Decide how much money to initially invest. You can always add more later. All investors need to contribute equally.

3. Formulate Portfolio

You need to create a brokerage account of your choice and have an operating agreement amongst your portfolio members.

4. Get Approved

Submit your capital amounts, brokerage account and operating agreement to Lucid. Once approved, you start investing!
